    Procedure to Study the VI characteristics of SCR

  • Set up the circuit on a breadboard by connecting the SCR, resistor, power supply, and multimeter as shown in the circuit diagram.
  • Turn on the power supply and adjust it to the desired voltage level. Measure the voltage using a multimeter.
  • Gradually increase the voltage while measuring the current through the SCR using the multimeter. Note down the values of voltage and current.
  • Repeat step 3 for different voltage levels, recording the corresponding current values.
